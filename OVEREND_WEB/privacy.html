Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

OVEREND is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your writing is personal and should remain private.

Data Collection

OVEREND operates primarily offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit your documents or personal data to external servers.

  • Documents: All your documents are stored locally on your device.
  • AI Processing: AI features use Apple's on-device intelligence; your data never leaves your Mac.
  • Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

What We May Collect

If you contact us for support, we may collect:

  • Your email address (to respond to your inquiry)
  • System information you choose to share for troubleshooting

Third-Party Services

OVEREND does not integrate with any third-party services that collect your personal data. The only external calls are for optional features like DOI lookup, which query public APIs without transmitting personal information.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
